About Us

HSR Consultancy Ltd, formed in 2016 and trading as Cynosure IT Consulting & Services is a professional services firm specialising in enterprise software solutions, technical consulting, and corporate training. We partner with businesses of all sizes to solve complex IT challenges, streamline digital transformation, and enhance workforce capabilities through targeted learning interventions.

With a team of seasoned consultants, engineers, and trainers, we offer scalable, high-impact solutions for cloud migration, performance testing, DevOps enablement, and custom application development. Our strategic collaborations with technology providers ensure our clients stay ahead in an ever-evolving digital landscape.
